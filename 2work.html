Her Editorial

Her is a comedy-drama film in which a solitary guy falls in love with the woman who "lives" inside his computer. I had a subject on University called "Morphology" which consign was take the essence of a film and bring it on a book. This design is about Samantha, the computer, and how her physic can be interpretated. I used a mix of graphic technics: traditional collage, digital collage, drawing and some other digital details as color saturation and effects.
